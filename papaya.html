Firt thing first, INGREDIENT 😀

  • Sliced green papaya
  • Sliced carrot
  • Peel shrimps
  • A hand full of chilli and garlic
  • 3 lime
  • Fresh tamarind sauce
  • A pinch of palm sugar
  • Fish sauce

Papaya... ummm, does it make your mouth watering??? It has that kind of effect for me everytime I mention about it. Ok, so let's started. First, we need to put sliced papaya and carrot together , mixed in a big bowl. Then add 3 table spoon of tamarind sauce, garlic, lime, 3 table spoon of fish sauce and sugar palm. Next, this is very important, add chilli, the more the better, trust me on this.

Mix them well and add shrimps. Mix one more time, do it with some power, you might need papaya sucks all the seasoning. OK, easy at that. It's ready to serve. See, not complicated, you have a fresh salad. The chilli crazily burn over your mouth, with some sweetness from palm sugar rescue the overheat.

Perfect combination. Heaven on earth guarantee!
